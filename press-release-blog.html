The SM Store Press Release The SM Store Wins at ICE

The SM Store wins at ice

January 21, 2020

3.6k SHARES Share Tweet

There’s more to The SM Store than being the Philippines’ go-to fashion haven as it proves to be at the forefront of innovative and groundbreaking style campaigns.

At the recently concluded ICE Awards spearheaded by the Media Specialists Association of the Philippines (MSAP), The SM Store bagged bronze distinctions for two campaigns under the Multimedia category: FashioniSM and SM Summer Stories.

FashioniSM featured a collaboration with Australia-based fashion influencer Margaret Zhang, and showcased online and offline marketing efforts. Meanwhile, SM Summer Stories highlighted some of the hottest fashion and beauty trends of the season for 2018.

The MSAP ICE Awards was held last November 6, 2019 at the Newport Performing Arts Theatre, Resorts World Manila, Pasay City.
